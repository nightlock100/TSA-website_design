Waffles Recipe:

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Neutral Oil
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • salt
  • Vanilla
  • Cooking spray
  • Waffle iron

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, add flour, milk and oil. Whisk in sugar. Stir.
  2. Pour batter into waffle iron. Cook until golden brown. Do the same with all the batter.
  3. Enjoy!
