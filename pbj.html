Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich Recipe:

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Fruit jam

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one bread slice.
  2. Spread the jelly on the other bread slice.
  3. Make it into a sandwich and eat.
