Pancakes Recipe:

Ingredients

  • garlic salt
  • chilli powder
  • onion powder
  • ground black pepper
  • jalepenos
  • 2 limes
  • 1 orange
  • 1 onion
  • boneless chicken breast

Steps

  1. Thaw out the chicken breast, and season with the first 5 items to your liking.
  2. Cut the limes and oranges in half. Cut the onion in circle slices. Soak the juices in the chicken.
  3. Grill the chicken!
  4. Enjoy!
