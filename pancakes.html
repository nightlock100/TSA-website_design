Pancakes Recipe:

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Combine fliur, baking powder, salt, and sugar into big bowl. Make well in center, and pour milk, oil, and egg. Mix.
  2. Put batter on a frying pan over medium heat/ Use 1/4 cup for each pancake. Wait until bubble form and dry edges. Flip and wait until brown on other side.
  3. Enjoy!
