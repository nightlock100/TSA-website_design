Pancakes Recipe:

Ingredients

  • 2.5 grams of salt
  • 10 grams baking soda
  • 50 grams Butter
  • 350 ml buttermilk
  • 50 grams sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 220 Grams Flour

Steps

  1. In a bowl mix together 220 grams of flour,2 tsp of baking powder, and half a tsp of baking soda.Next add 50 grams of sugar and half a tsp of salt. In another bowl mix 350 ml of butter milk and 3 eggs.
  2. Create a small whole and add the milk and egg mixture.Stirl the mixture until fully together and add 2 oz of melted butter.Make sure not to over mix the batter. Remember it's ok to have lumps.
  3. Add oil to the pan over medium high heat. Add 1/4 of the mixture to the pan.
  4. Mix the marinara sauce with the meat and stir. Set stove to low.
