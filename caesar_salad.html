Caesar Salad Recipe:

Ingredients

  • 1 Large head romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup shaved parmesan
  • A pack of croutons
  • 1 Bottle Caesar dressing

Steps

  1. Wash romaine, cut into bite-size pieces, and dry.
  2. Add croutons, parmesan, and caesar sauce. Mix it very good!
  3. Enjoy your healthy lunch!
