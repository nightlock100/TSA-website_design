Spaghetti Recipe:

Ingredients

  • A box of spaghetti
  • Marinara Sauce
  • 1-2 pounds of ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 2 big pots
  • Utensils
  • salt
  • water

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water and add some salt. Boil it.
  2. Take the meat and put it in a large pan, put on stove, set to medium.
  3. Break meat into smaller pieces. Then, flip the meat to make sure it cooks evenly. Takes 10-15 to cook.
  4. Mix the marinara sauce with the meat and stir. Set stove to low.
  5. But the spaghetti in the boiling water for 7-11 minutes. Stir every few minutes.
  6. Combine and serve!
